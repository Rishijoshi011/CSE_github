3. Explain the relationship between Data Science and Big Data. What are the key characteristics (6Vs) of Big Data?

Data Science and Big Data are closely related, but they serve different purposes:

ğŸ”¹ Big Data refers to large, complex datasets that traditional data processing tools cannot handle efficiently. It involves storing, processing, and managing vast amounts of structured, semistructured, and unstructured data.

ğŸ”¹ Data Science is the field that extracts meaningful insights from data using statistics, machine learning, and analytics. It helps in understanding and making sense of Big Data.

ğŸ”— Relationship:
Big Data provides the raw material, while Data Science applies tools and techniques to extract valuable insights from it. Without Data Science, Big Data is just a vast collection of information with no useful meaning.
